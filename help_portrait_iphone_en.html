How can I send these animated Emojis?

They can be send via iMessage,MMS or Email.

With iMessage you can send these animated Emojis to iPhone, iPad,iPod Touch and Mac.

With MMS youc an send these animated Emojis to other cell phones. Please note that the using of MMS can be extra charged by your cell phone carrier.

How to insert these animated Emojis in the Messages APP?

1. Tap emoticon what you like.

2. Select "Text Messages".

3. Choose the recipient(must have iMessage or MMS)

4.Tap and hold message field to Paste".

Can these animated Emojis be send via normal text messages?

NO, they only can be send with iMessage or MMS.

I can't insert these emojis into my text messaging input field.

Either iMessage or MMS have to be activated in your phone settings.

Can I use these animated Emojis with WhatsApp or other messaging Apps

Yes, these animated emojis can be used with other messaging services too. But most of these services send these animated Emojis without animations.

Can I add these animated Emojis to system keyboard?

Sorry, all emojis can't be add to system keyboard.
